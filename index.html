About Us

Inspired from the #1 New York Times bestseller, HONY, Humans Of IIT Jammu is an attempt to capture the family of IIT Jammu and their stories, all to be combined in a vibrant blog and possibly a book that will be published soon. We aim to bring stories of achievements, failures, love, abuse, perspective, intellect and insanity. Stories that stand out fearless and raw for everyone to read and the privileged to feel.
